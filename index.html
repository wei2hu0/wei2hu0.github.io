Bio

I'm Wei Zhuo, a research fellow at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), working with Prof. Han Yu. I am also a member of Joint NTU-WeBank Research Centre on Fintech. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science at Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU), supervised by Prof. Guang Tan. I was a research intern at the National University of Singapore (NUS), supervised by Prof. Bingsheng He. During my time at NUS, I also collaborated with Prof. Bryan Hooi and Prof. Zemin Liu. Before that, I was a visiting student at the University of Helsinki, where I was supervised by Prof. Jiaheng Lu.

My recent research interests focus on, but not limited to foundation models, federated learning, graph machine learning, and AI for science (GraphAI for drugs/molecules/math/healthcare/materials/social science).
